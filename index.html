Great location

Palmela Village is a touristic development located in Quinta do Anjo, 40 km South of Lisbon, in the district of Setúbal, a dynamic coastal town. It is served by excellent infrastructures and close to all the amenities you can wish for...

Photo: Sesimbra, in the Setubal district, by A. Rotenberg

Culture & nature

The village benefits from the proximity of the Castles of Palmela, great beaches (9 km away) and the beautiful landscapes of the Serra da Arrábida Natural Park.

Photo: Palmela Castle, courtersy of IAD Portugal

Safe environment

The housing development is fenced, with security-controlled entrance and exit points. It is integrated in a landscaped park, which offers plenty of opportunities for exercise or casual walks, in one of the most beautiful natural areas on the coast of Lisbon. There is a communal outdoor swimming pool and a fine restaurant on site.
